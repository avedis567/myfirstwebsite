Frequently Asked Questions

What's your turnaround time?

Standard orders are completed in 3-5 business days. Rush and express services are available for urgent needs with same-day delivery possible.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer competitive bulk discounts for orders over 500 pieces. Contact us for specific pricing.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept all common file formats including PDF, JPG, PNG, TIFF, and AI files. For large files, we recommend using our file upload service.

Do you deliver?

Yes, we offer local delivery throughout the San Francisco Bay Area and shipping nationwide for larger orders.

Can I see samples before printing?

Absolutely! We provide digital proofs for approval before proceeding with large orders.
